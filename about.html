About Jnicc

Who We Are

The Julius Nyerere International Convetion Center (JNICC) is a Premier Venue for national and International conference, Exhbitions, and events in Tanzania. It stand as symbol of unity, dialogue, and Developments, businesses, and global Organizations.

Our Governance

JNICC operates under the stewardship of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and East African Cooperation of the United Republic of Tanzania. Through this Leadership, The center plays a vital role in fostering regional integration, diplomacy, and global partenerships.

Our Mission

To provide World conference services that support sustainable Development, diplomatic engagement, and knowledge exchange in Tanzania and across the African continent.
